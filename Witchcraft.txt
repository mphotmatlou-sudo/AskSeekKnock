WITCHCRAFT

One Hour Itemized Prayer Session

60min – 5min/item

These prayer points have been thoughtfully crafted to empower Christians,
both young and old, to cultivate a consistent, dynamic, and impactful prayer

life.

Through their consistent use, believers will grow in faith, experience tangible
results, and learn to wield their God-given authority in Jesus Christ, causing
them to triumph over the spiritual forces of darkness in the heavenly realms.
___________________________________________________________________

______________
PREAMBLE
Ephesians 6: 10-12

Finally, be strong in the Lord and in his mighty power. Put on the full armor of
God, so that you can take your stand against the devil’s schemes. For our
struggle is not against flesh and blood, but against the rulers, against the
authorities, against the powers of this dark world and against the spiritual

forces of evil in the heavenly realms.

The purpose of this itemized prayer session is to confront the witches and
spiritual forces of wickedness that have positioned themselves against you
and your family with the authority you have been given in Christ through the

victory of the finished works of the cross.

WARFARE
Psalm 35: 1-3

Contend, Lord, with those who contend with me; fight against those who fight
against me. Take up shield and armor; arise and come to my aid. Brandish
spear and javelin against those who pursue me. Say to me, “I am your

salvation.”

Ask God to fight on you and your family’s behalf in the name of the Lord Jesus

Christ.
WARFARE
Luke 10: 19

I have given you authority to trample on snakes and scorpions and to
overcome all the power of the enemy; nothing will harm you.

Take authority over every demon, devil and evil spirit that has been assigned
to oppose your life and bring them under the authority and victory of the Lord

Jesus Christ.
WARFARE
Luke 12: 3

There is nothing concealed that will not be disclosed, or hidden that will not be
made known. What you have said in the dark will be heard in the daylight, and
what you have whispered in the ear in the inner rooms will be proclaimed from

the roofs.

Ask God to expose all the witches, warlocks, wizards, sorcerers, diviners,
necromancers and enchanters, and every spell they have spoken over you and

your family.
WARFARE
Matthew 16: 19

I will give you the keys of the kingdom of heaven; and whatever you bind
(declare to be improper and unlawful) on earth must be what is already bound
in heaven; and whatever you loose (declare lawful) on earth must be what is

already loosed in heaven. [AMPC]

Bind and break every curse, every spell, every hex, every enchantment, every
incantation and every divination that has been spoken against you and your
family. Declare them improper and unlawful, and loosen yourself and your

family from the bondage of these spells.

WARFARE
Matthew 18: 18

Truly I tell you, whatever you bind on earth will be bound in heaven, and

whatever you loose on earth will be loosed in heaven.
Bind every witch, every warlock, every wizard, every sorcerer, diviner,
enchanter and necromancer that has been pronouncing curses against you
and your family, and loosen yourself and your family from the chains, shackles
and tethers tying you to these evil works and workers of witchcraft.

WARFARE
Psalm 91: 11-13

For he will command his angels concerning you to guard you in all your ways;
they will lift you up in their hands, so that you will not strike your foot against
a stone. You will tread on the lion and the cobra; you will trample the great lion

and the serpent.

Bind, blind, disable and dismember every familiar spirit operating in you and
your family’s lives, every monitoring spirit sent to keep tabs and give reports
on the lives of you and your family and every spiritual spouse attached to you
and your family members. Ask God to command HIS holy angels to war
against all the monitoring, familiar and spiritual spouse spirits assigned to

you.
WARFARE
Deuteronomy 12: 2-3

Destroy completely all the places on the high mountains, on the hills and
under every spreading tree, where the nations you are dispossessing worship
their gods. Break down their altars, smash their sacred stones and burn their
Asherah poles in the fire; cut down the idols of their gods and wipe out their

names from those places.

Uproot and destroy every demonic altar in the heavens, on earth and under the
earth that has been erected against you and your family. Bind and break every
blood covenant and bloodline bend that has been keeping you and your family

in generational bondage.
WARFARE

Matthew 12: 29 &amp; Numbers 23: 23

Or again, how can anyone enter a strong man’s house and carry off his
possessions unless he first ties up the strong man? Then he can plunder his

house.
&amp;

There is no divination against Jacob, no evil omens against Israel. It will now

be said of Jacob and of Israel, ‘See what God has done!

Bind and dispossess every strong man, power, principality, ruler, authority,
demon and devil from the kingdom of darkness that has been assigned to
yoke you and your family. Break every demonic cycle that has been hindering

your progress.
WARFARE
Psalm 35: 5-6 &amp; 26

May they be like chaff before the wind, with the angel of the Lord driving them
away; may their path be dark and slippery, with the angel of the Lord pursuing

them.
&amp;

May all who gloat over my distress be put to shame and confusion; may all
who exalt themselves over me be clothed with shame and disgrace.
Ask God to command HIS holy angels to locate, scatter and destroy forever,
every fetish and every altar of every gathering and coven of witchcraft that has

been fashioned as a weapon against you and your family.

WARFARE
Luke 10: 18-19

He replied, “I saw Satan fall like lightning from heaven. I have given you
authority to trample on snakes and scorpions and to overcome all the power of

the enemy; nothing will harm you.

Command, in the authority of the name of the Lord Jesus Christ, every open
doorway and portal that has been exploited by any demons, devils, spiritual
spouses and strongmen to harass and oppress you and your family to be shut

and sealed forever by the fire of the Holy Spirit.

WARFARE

Exodus 22: 18 &amp; Genesis 12: 3
Thou shalt not suffer a witch to live.

&amp;

I will bless those who bless you, and whoever curses you I will curse; and all

peoples on earth will be blessed through you.”

Pronounce God&#39;s curse of death upon every witch, every warlock, every
wizard, every sorcerer, every diviner, necromancer and enchanter that has
positioned themselves against you and your family’s blessings and prophetic

destinies in Christ.
WARFARE

Colossians 2: 15 &amp; Corinthians 2: 14

And having disarmed the powers and authorities, he made a public spectacle

of them, triumphing over them by the cross.

&amp;

But thanks be to God, who always leads us as captives in Christ’s triumphal
procession and uses us to spread the aroma of the knowledge of him

everywhere.

Thank God for the victory you have in Christ over all the witches, witchcraft
and demonization that have been assailing and plaguing you and your family.
Decree and declare that you are victorious over every situation and

circumstance that has been fashioned as a weapon against you and your

family.

THANKSGIVING CONFESSION

Exodus 14: 13-14

Moses answered the people, “Do not be afraid. Stand firm and you will see the
deliverance the Lord will bring you today. The Egyptians you see today you
will never see again. The Lord will fight for you; you need only to be still.”
Thank God for HIS promise to fight for you and to deliver you from every
single one of your enemies and oppressors as you stand firm in your faith in

Christ’s victory over sin and death.

HALLELUYAH!
